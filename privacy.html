Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 29, 2025

Type-R 198X is a single-player typing shooter published by Christopher Bradford. This policy describes what data the web build, iOS/iPadOS build (including Mac Catalyst), and macOS build collect and how that data is used.

What We Collect

  • Gameplay preferences stored locally. Pilot name, ship selection, unlocked levels, and settings (mute/CRT toggles) are saved inside the browser or the embedded WKWebView via localStorage. The data never leaves your device.
  • Run progress cache. If you pause mid-run, the game stores your current stats in the same local storage namespace (typesmup198x.save.v1) so you can continue later.
  • No personal identifiers. We do not collect email addresses, analytics IDs, IP-based telemetry, or crash reports.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account creation or authentication.
  • No network calls to third-party APIs while you play.
  • No advertising SDKs, attribution trackers, or fingerprinting techniques.

Offline-First Design

The native apps bundle the entire web experience and run it offline through WKWebView (iOS/iPadOS) or WKWebView on macOS. All assets and scripts ship inside the binary. An internet connection is only required for initial download, TestFlight updates, or when you explicitly visit typer198x.com.

Your Controls

  • You can reset all locally stored data from the in-game Options menu ("Reset Save"), which clears the localStorage key.
  • You may also delete the app or clear your browser storage to remove all data.

Children

The game is rated for ages 9+ (mild fantasy violence). We intentionally avoid collecting data from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to contact@typer198x.com.
