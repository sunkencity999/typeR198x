Type-R 198X

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

No Data Collection

Type-R 198X does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All gameplay data (ship selection, progress, preferences) remains on your device inside local storage solely to enable your own save files.

No Account Storage

We do not host accounts or store passwords. When a macOS or web version prompts for a name, it is only used locally to personalize your pilot card. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Type-R 198X currently ships exclusively through platform storefronts (such as Apple’s). We do not embed ads, analytics SDKs, or payment processors, and we rely on each storefront’s existing privacy protections for purchases and distribution.

Your Controls

  • Clear your browser’s local storage to remove saved runs/settings.
  • Uninstall the app to delete all data on macOS/iOS.
  • Contact support@typer198x.com with any questions.

Updates

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and provide the revised statement on this page.
